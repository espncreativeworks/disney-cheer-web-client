Routine Guidelines

Don't forget to read the official rules for complete details.

Do's

Do

create a fun and spirited Disney Parks inspired routine showing how you feel when you visit one of the Parks. Show Us Your Disney Side!

Do

use your creativity, this is not a skills based competition

Do

use only one track of the music provided keeping your routine to :30 seconds. Timing begins with the first movement, voice or note of the music

Do

participate if you are a recognized/official team of a College, University or Junior College in the United States. (No club or competition-only teams)

Do

wear your school uniforms. Show your school spirit!

Do

follow UDA Summer Camp Guidelines if you are a dance team.

Dont's

Don't

exceed 20 members per team, don't include stunts or pyramids over prep level or basket tosses of any kind

Don't

include any members on your squad that are currently on scholarship for any sport
